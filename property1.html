Description:

This character filled Silvermine colonial offers picturesque views of a scenic pond at the base of the river. Originally built in the 20s, the home features a great family room built off formal living room with fireplace plus a sunken den with stone floors and gas fireplace. Both the eat in kitchen and formal dining room overlook a two tier deck with serene water views. There is a legal studio apartment over the detached two car garage currently rented. A unique offering for those looking for a home with charm.
